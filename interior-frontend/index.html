About Us

Sri Vekkaliyamman Interiors is a Trichy-based interior design and custom woodwork company. We create beautiful kitchens, wardrobes, pooja rooms, furniture and complete home interiors — designed for your lifestyle, built for a lifetime.

What makes us different is the personal attention we give to every project. We don't just design spaces — we listen to your ideas, understand your needs, and bring them to life with care and precision. Every detail matters to us, from the smallest finish to the overall look of your home.

With over 15 years of experience in interior design and woodwork, we have built a strong reputation for quality and reliability. Our work combines traditional craftsmanship with modern design, ensuring that every piece we create is both stylish and long-lasting.

Our goal is simple — to make your home more comfortable, beautiful, and meaningful. Whether it's a small change or a complete interior transformation, we are committed to delivering results that you and your family will love for years to come.
